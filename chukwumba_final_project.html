Question 1

Which two parent companies produce the most plastics in Argentina?

Methods

plastics %>%
  filter(country == "Argentina") %>%
  slice(1:10) %>%
   ggplot(aes(x = grand_total,
              y = parent_company)) +
  geom_col(color = "grey0",
           fill = "cyan") +
  theme_classic() +
  labs(x = "Total (plastics)",
       y = "Parent Company")

Answer

Unbranded and Grand Total



Question 2

Does The Coca-Cola Company produce more plastics than PepsiCo in the United States?

Methods

plastics %>%
  filter(country == "United States of America") %>%
  slice(1:10) %>%
  distinct() %>%
   ggplot(aes(x = grand_total,
              y = parent_company)) +
  geom_col(color = "red2",
           fill = "dodgerblue2") + 
  labs(x = "Total (plastics)",
       y = "Parent Company") + 
  theme_classic()

Answer

No, they do not.
